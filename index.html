About

I am a Dr 2 — I have a medical degree and a doctorate in clinical neurosciences. Currently, I work as a Medical Director and support Neuroscience Clinical Development at Eli Lilly.

Throughout my career, I have been developing new treatments through clinical trials, imaging, and the use of big data. I have also published on improving reliability of patient-reported outcome measures by minimising bias, placebo or nocebo response and by including functional outcomes and biomarkers.

I run, sail, dive, and take photos.
