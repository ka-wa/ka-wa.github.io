About

I am a Dr 2 — both medical and by degree.

I work on developing new treatments for neurological conditions; through, clinical trials, neuroimaging methods and big data.

I am interested in improving the methods of analysis of subjective outcomes, including the placebo and nocebo response, and longitudinal outcome assessment.

I run, sail, dive, and take photos of birds. On rainy days, I try to cook from an actual recipe, procrastinate on Steam and sometimes try to improve my website.
