CLINICAL STUDIES
  1. Oxford Cognitive Comorbidity and Ageing Research (ORCHARD). A cohort study aiming to identify older hospital patients at high short-term risk of dementia and cognitive decline using routinely collected hospital electronic clinical and brain imaging data to improve care. Funded by NIHR i4i programme grant (NIHR 204290) on which I am a co-applicant. Study website / NIHR website
  2. Oxford Haemodynamic Adaptation to Reduce Pulsatility trial (OxHARP). A double-cross-over placebo controlled randomised clinical trial on the effects of two phosphodiesterase inhibitors (Sildenafil and Cilostazol) on cerebrovascular reactivity and the pulsatility of cerebral blood flow. Funded by Wellcome Trust.about ClinicalTrials.gov: NCT03855332 / Protocol / Results
  3. Effects of Amlodipine and Other Blood Pressure Lowering Agents on Microvascular Function trial (TREAT@SVDs). A phase three cross-over trial of the effects of three commonly used anti-hypertensives (Amlodipine, Losartan, Atenolol) on endothelial function and cerebrovascular reactivity. Funded by the European Union’s Horizon 2020 programme. ClinicalTrials.gov: NCT03082014 / Protocol / Results
  4. Oxford Vascular Study (OxVASC). A cohort study of people at risk of stroke and vascular dementia. Study website .
  5. Arterial Pulsatility in Small Vessel Diseases – Measurement of Arterial Pulsatility on MRI (OxBP). A sub-study of the TREAT@SVDs trial investigating the effects of vasodilators on cerebral pulsatility.
