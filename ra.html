"Understanding Pain Processing in Chronic Pain Using Neuroimaging Tools"

A trial investigating structural and functional MRI changes in patients with severe rheumatoid arthritis treated with monoclonal antibodies against tumour necrosis factor; funded by GlaxoSmithKline.

I led the trial, including study management, clinical assessment, data acquisition and analysis and preparation of study reports and publications.

Thesis; Structural analysis; Clinical, psychobehavioural, BOLD-FMRI analysis.
